ADVENTURES

ADVENTURE TIME!

Apart from its breathtakingly beautiful landscapes, Interlaken is also a paradise for all adventure sports enthusiasts. In fact, the list of these thrill-inducing activities is quite unending when it comes to this resort town and is therefore the top Swiss destination for adventure sports. Get your adrenaline pumping as you bungee jump into the unknown. Experience your heart racing with excitement as you go sky diving above the massive mountains. Let bliss wash over you as you enjoy a bird’s eye view of emerald lakes and verdant pastures, all while paragliding with your ever-so-protective guide. You could embark on a trek across some of the longest glaciers in Europe and cycle along meadows and hills that are perpetually alive with the sound of music. Nothing will stop you from channelling your inner Tiger Woods with a nice session of golf and once you’re done, you can explore the charming streets of the town on a Segway! And if you still haven’t had enough, Interlaken also has a kart track of its own for you to go wild.

Do keep in mind that these activities require prior reservations. Expect to spend anything around 500 CHF per person, depending on the sport. The memories you will go back home with are worth all the money.

BIKING

Biking adventure is an incredible way to explore the world around us. It is an activity that can be enjoyed by people of all ages, and it offers a unique and exciting way to experience nature, culture, and the outdoors. Whether you're a seasoned cyclist or a beginner, a biking adventure can be an unforgettable experience.

The thrill of exploring new places on a bike is unparalleled. You can take in the sights, sounds, and smells of your surroundings in a way that you simply cannot experience from a car or bus. You can go at your own pace, and stop to take photos or rest whenever you feel like it. Biking also provides a great workout, so you can stay fit while enjoying the scenery.

PARAGLIDING

Paragliding is an exhilarating adventure sport that has gained immense popularity in recent years. It is a thrilling activity that allows you to soar through the air like a bird, and experience breathtaking views from high up in the sky. Paragliding is an adventure that can be enjoyed by people of all ages, and it is a unique way to experience the outdoors.

When embarking on a paragliding adventure, it is important to find a reputable company that provides paragliding services. You should ensure that the company you choose has experienced and certified pilots, and that they use quality equipment. Safety should always be your top priority, and you should never compromise on it.

SURFING

Surfing is a thrilling adventure sport that has been enjoyed by people all over the world for centuries. It is a sport that requires a lot of skill, balance, and patience, but the rewards are well worth the effort. Surfing is an activity that can be enjoyed by people of all ages and skill levels, and it offers a unique way to experience the ocean and the outdoors.

When embarking on a surfing adventure, it is important to find a reputable surf school or instructor that can teach you the basics of surfing. Surfing requires a lot of practice, and it is important to learn proper technique and safety measures before taking to the waves. You should also make sure that you have the right equipment, including a surfboard and wetsuit that are suitable for your skill level and the conditions of the waves.

SKYDIVING

Skydiving is an adventure sport that involves jumping from an airplane at high altitude and freefalling through the sky before deploying a parachute and gliding back to the ground. It is an exhilarating experience that provides a rush of adrenaline and a unique perspective on the world below.

Before taking part in a skydiving adventure, you must receive proper training and certification from a reputable skydiving school. This training will teach you the basic skills and safety measures needed for skydiving, including proper technique for exiting the airplane, controlling your body in freefall, and deploying your parachute.
